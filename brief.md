# Project Brief

## Problem
People are not sure how to optimise their space for productivity. They want structured information on how their room is impeding or promoting productivity and actionable steps on how to optimise it. 

# Target user
Business owners wanting an office refresh, particularly small business owners who wouldn't pay money for a large interior design project. Market in Asian countries for feng shui design. 

## Product
An app/website which, given a floor plan (or photo of a room), creates an interactive floor plan where furniture can be moved around. A score is given for the productivity rating of the room based on parameters like lighting, air quality, and traffic flow with a breakdown of each aspect provided. Recommendations are given for floor plans. The user can input custom requirements (e.g. feng shui analysis, collaborative space, individual space) which will alter the criteria.

There is also a 2.5D model of the room which gives the user a better idea of what the different layouts will look like, and a 3D first-person walkthrough. The user can try different wall colours and floor textures, and add in 3D models of real furniture from a database of affiliate links. Wall colours and floor textures could also be affiliate links (Resene, Bunnings etc.). A budget is created as the user works. 

## Financial model
It is a subscription-based service with a demo video and a one-time subscription to use it for one office space. Affiliate links also provide revenue.
