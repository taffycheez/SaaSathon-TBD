# Research

## Feng shui
- Command position: desks should see door w/o being directly in line with it
- Positive energy flow (chi) requires reducing clutter
- Rounded edges (on desks etc.) to encourage flow of energy
- Incorporate 5 elements
    - Fire: Lighting; red/orange accents
    - Wood: Plants, wooden furniture
    - Earth: Stone, ceramics, yellow/beige
    - Metal: Metal furnishings, white/grey
    - Water: Aquariums, blue/black shades
- Avoid fluorescent lighting
- Artificial lighting = yang energy (creativity and activity)
- Darkness = yin energy (resting and thinking)
- Back of office chair shouldn't be behind chair/window
- Supportive, full-back chairs
- Keep walkways clear

## Competitors

**Individual use**
- RoomFlow AI - give design options based on image
- FengShuiModern AI - suggests optimized layout given floor plan ($20)
- LumenFeng - create 3D space or floor plan and feng shui it up. Also provides analysis based on circadian rhythm and safety 
- AI Feng Shui - using 1 photo or floor plan sketch
- Planner 5D - create floor plan and upload furniture from a catalogue

**Corporate use**
- RoomSketcher - transforms photo scan to floor plan (or draw floor plans) and creates editable 3D floor plan with finishes and wall colours, furniture etc.
- Spacely - given photo can change it to look different, generate realistic photos, editing specific components (more specific than just GPT)
- Planner 5D - generates a 3d model from a 2d floor plan, suggests furniture placement and layout options, create floor plan from single image, VR walkthrough
- Laiout - upload floor plan, reconfigures based on regulations, custom furniture library, 3D walkthroughs and visualisers.
